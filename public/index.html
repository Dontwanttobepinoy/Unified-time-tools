DST Information

Daylight Saving Time (DST) is the practice of setting clocks forward one hour from standard time during the summer months.

2024 DST Schedule:
  • Spring Forward: March 10, 2024 (US/Canada)
  • Fall Back: November 3, 2024 (US/Canada)
  • Europe Spring: March 31, 2024
  • Europe Fall: October 27, 2024
